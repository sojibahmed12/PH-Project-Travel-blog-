WELCOME TO 360° TRAVEL INSPIRATIONS!

Imagine waking up to the sound of waves gently crashing against white sandy shores, stepping out of your water villa, and diving straight into crystal-clear turquoise waters. That’s Maldives – a tropical dream made real.

Located in the Indian Ocean, the Maldives is a collection of 1,190+ coral islands grouped into 26 atolls. It's famous for:

  • Overwater bungalows
  • Colorful coral reefs & marine life
  • Delicious seafood & tropical fruits
  • Romantic sunsets & luxury resorts

The Food I had taste

  • Fihunu Mas – Grilled fish, usually tuna or reef fish, marinated with chili and spices.
  • Mas Huni – Shredded smoked tuna mixed with coconut, onions, and chili. Eaten with flatbread (roshi) for breakfast.
  • Maldivian Fish Curry (Mas Riha) – A spicy tuna curry with coconut milk, onions, garlic, and curry leaves.
  • Kulhi Boakibaa – A spicy fish cake made from smoked tuna, coconut, and rice.
